Logical operators:
	! (logical NOT)
	|| (logical OR)
	&& (logical AND)

Bitwise operators:
	& (bitwise AND)
	| (bitwise OR)
	^ (bitwise XOR)
	&^ (bit clear, AND NOT)
	<< (left shift)
	>> (right shift)

Comparison operators:
	== (equal)
	!= (not equal)
	< (less than)
	<= (less than or equal to)
	> (greater than)
	>= (greater than or equal to)
